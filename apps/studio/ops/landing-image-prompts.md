# Landing Image Prompt Pack

These are the curated high-detail prompt directions for the next landing image refresh. They are written to match the required structure in one sentence each: scene, composition, light, material detail, camera language, and quality target. The goal is not random variety; it is a premium atmosphere set with controlled contrast and strong cinematic realism.

## Visual Standard

- premium editorial / film-still quality
- physically believable light and shadow
- strong off-center composition
- crisp surface detail, no muddy blur
- no fake text, no signage, no UI, no stock-photo vibe
- works behind landing copy without killing readability

## Acceptance Checklist

Reject any result that has:

- muddy blur or melted detail
- generic AI face or plastic skin
- centered object syndrome
- flat lighting
- fake signage, fake text, or noisy props
- stock-template composition
- contrast so high that landing copy would die on top

Keep only images that feel like premium campaign plates, not demo outputs.
